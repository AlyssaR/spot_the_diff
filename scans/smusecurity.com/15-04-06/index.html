Home

The Security Special Interest Group (SSIG) is a student-led group of passionate students – primarily undergraduates –  at SMU interested in learning more about all aspects of security.

SSIG holds weekly meetings with talks and demos by members on various topics in security – offensive to defensive, cyber to physical. SSIG is also a regular competitor at the Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC) and participates in other competitions and capture-the-flags (CTF) throughout the year.

This website is intended to be a collection of resources for both SSIG members and the general public. Notes from talks and general resources will be added periodically.

 

If you are an SMU student interested in participating, please check out our meetings (details on the Calendar).

For SSIG news, follow us on Twitter @smu_ssig.

For general security news, follow this account on Twitter @SMUcurity where we try to aggregate interesting, helpful, and funny articles from security professionals.
